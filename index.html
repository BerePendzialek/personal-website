Hola! I am an international, experienced tourism professional starting in the web development world, having fun and always looking for new challenges

Biography

Previous to my web development turn, I worked as tourism consultant from 2005 to 2019, with focus on Chinese outbound tourism to the Americas and Europe.
Through the years, I have gathered experience on performing qualitative and quantitative research and analysis, online marketing and social media, international project management,
keynote presentations and trainings to tourism stakeholders, as well as event organization.

In 2020, I wanted to start something new and noticed that I enjoyed programming. I started with the book “Java for Dummies"
and went on to watch some tutorials from Wes Bos about Java Script. After that, I learned about the web development bootcamp at neue fische and here I am working my way through code, apps and enjoying the time spent with my classmates and coaches.

Originally from Culiacan, Mexico, I currently live in Hamburg, Germany. In my free time, I enjoy riding my road bike (have a look at my Strava profile to see my tours),
travelling and be in nice company.
