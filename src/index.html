Hola! I am an international, experienced tourism professional starting in the web development world, having fun and always looking for new challenges

Biography

Previous to my web development turn, I devoted myself from 2005 to 2019 to Chinese tourism, especially the topic of outbound tourism to the Americas and Europe.
Through the years, I have gathered experience on performing qualitative and quantitative research and analysis, online marketing and social media, international project management,
keynote presentations and trainings to tourism stakeholders, as well as event organization.

In 2020, I wanted to start something new. I needed a break from tourism and noticed that programming was such a fun thing to do. I started with the book “Java for Dummies"
and went on to watch some tutorials from Wes Bos about Java Script. After that I got a recommendation of neue fische and here I am studying to become a web developer, working my way through code, apps and enjoying the time spent with my classmates and coaches.

Originally from Culiacan, Mexico, I currently live in Hamburg, Germany. I just love to be moving, I enjoy riding my road bike (have a look at my Strava profile to see my tours),
travelling and assisting to meet ups. Here you can find some photos that go along with my biography of mobility.
