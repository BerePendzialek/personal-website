Biography

Previous to my web development turn, I devoted myself from 2005 to 2019 to Chinese tourism, especially the topic of outbound tourism to the Americas and Europe. Through the years, I have gathered experience on performing qualitative and quantitative research and analysis, online marketing and social media, international project management, keynote presentations and trainings to industry´s stakeholders, as well as event organization.

In 2020, I wanted to start something new. I needed a break from tourism and noticed that programming was such a fun thing to do. I started with a book “Java for Dummies'' and went on to watch some tutorials from Wes Bos about Java Script. After that I got a recommendation of neue fische and here I am working my way through code, apps and enjoying the time spent with my classmates and coaches.

Originally from Culiacan, Mexico, I currently live in Hamburg, Germany. I just love to be moving, I enjoy riding my road bike (have a look at my Strava profile to see my tours), travelling and assisting to meet ups. Here you can find some photos that go along with my biography of mobility.
